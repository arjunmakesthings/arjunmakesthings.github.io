bric-a-brac

2026

collective musical instrument,
at nyu.

for sound-studio, a class taught by luke dubois & craig fahner in spring-2026 at nyu, 16 students were asked to contribute two sound-producing objects to make a collective-instrument that could be triggered by a 5v input.

i made a sound-sculpture with found objects & an analog-circuit to produce a frequency that feeds back on itself, and a keyboard that replicates the sound of fingers tapping with solenoids.







the making is extensively documented here.
