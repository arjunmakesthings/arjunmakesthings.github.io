how do i spend my days?

2022

independent experiment,
data visualisation.

How we spend our days is, of course, how we spend our lives.


inspired by annie dillard's eloquent words, i sought a way to keep track of how i spend my life. there were six distinct buckets that i wanted to spend my time in:

  1. work (my full-time job)
  2. teaching (my part-time job)
  3. creative practice
  4. people (friends, family & social obligations)
  5. health (physical exercise)
  6. archiving (website & writing activities)

the objective was to achieve a balance every day, and put time into each one of them. the amount of time didn't matter — (a) because some buckets (such as working out) required lesser time than others (my full-time job), and (b) the amount of time i spent in a bucket might vary because of the situation at-hand.
