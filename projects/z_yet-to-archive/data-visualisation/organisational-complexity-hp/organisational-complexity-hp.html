Context

During my first month at TinkerLabs, Vaibhavi & Abhi approached me with an interesting challenge: their team had been working on a project with HP and needed to communicate how instruction from senior members of the management gets lost in the sea of employees below them in the hierarchical ladder.

They had managed to procure data on the hierarchical chain and were trying different visualisations to communicate the scale.


A visualisation that Abhi had tried, but was unhappy with.

I wrote a custom program for them where they could input the names of the managers & leads and show how information is disseminated to the employees under them.
