just ask

2022

chatbot design,
done at tinkerlabs,
for unfpa.

unfpa approached tinkerlabs to conceptualise & design an ai-enabled chatbot, with the intention of improving sexual-health awareness amongst adolescents in low-resource regions of india. i helped conduct design-research, and led conversation-design, with a 4-person team.

within 2 months of its launch in madhya-pradesh, 25, 000+ unique adolescents had interacted with it.

through design-research visits to villages in madhya pradesh, we established information-seeking patterns in adolescents — the triggers, sources and outcomes. over time — and with internet-data — we concluded that there were 8 emotional needs that adolescents were trying to meet, by going to unreliable sources for sexual-health queries.

via a technology partner, we understood the technical limitations of whatsapp, and proposed 173 feature, tonal and identity ideas for the chatbot. i then prepared a wizard-of-oz system that enabled human-researchers to process & respond to open-ended text messages, just like an ai-enabled chatbot would in the future.

we then went back to madhya-pradesh, watched adolescents fulfil information-seeking tasks via our wizard-of-oz system, and conducted a semantic-differential-survey to arrive at our final design considerations for the chatbot.

in 2023, unfpa launched the just ask / khulke poocho chatbot, with the madhya-pradesh government.
