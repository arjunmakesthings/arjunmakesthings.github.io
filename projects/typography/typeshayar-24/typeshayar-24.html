generative graphics

Context

I made some generative visuals for Vikram — the organiser of the first 'TypeShayar' in 2024. TypeShayar was meant to be a virtual "festival of letterforms and the words they create, of calligraphy and type and everything in between."

I initially reached out to exhibit my work, but ended up recycling a couple of my earlier programs to create loops that could be played in-between sessions.
