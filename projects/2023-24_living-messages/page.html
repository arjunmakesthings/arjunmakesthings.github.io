living messages

2023 - 24

generative expressive typography,
self-led experiments.

inspired by the phrase — the medium is the massage (by marshall mcluhan) — i began exploring the idea of ‘living’ typographic messages, made with code.

the idea was to communicate a written message in a way where its visual-display aids in its communication, as opposed to writing and sharing expressive pieces of thought using nondescript typefaces.
